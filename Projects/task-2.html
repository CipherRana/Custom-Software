Dr.Norman Borloug

Dr.Norman Borloug,tried from the left trains biologists in Mexico on how to increase wheat yields part of his lifelong war on hunger

Here's a time line of Dr.Borloug's life:

  • 1914- Born in Cresca,Iowa.
  • 1933- Leaves his family farm to attend the University of Minnesota,thanks to Depression era program known as "National Youth Administration".
  • 1935- Has to stop school and save up more money.Works in the Civilian Conservaton Corps helping starving Americans.
